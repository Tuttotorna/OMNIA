## Structural Observability

Structural Observability is the measurement of residual structure before inference, meaning, and decision.

Its core question is:

~~~text
when I stop trusting the form in which something appears,
what structure remains measurable?
~~~

Its central boundary is:

~~~text
measurement != inference != decision
~~~

For the MB-X.01 / L.O.N. ecosystem:

~~~text
measurement != validation != orchestration != decision
~~~

## What this repository measures

- structural coherence
- residual invariance
- fragility
- irreversibility
- measurement-only evidence

## Boundary

~~~text
OMNIA measures structural behavior after an output, trace, or artifact already exists. It does not infer meaning, learn, or decide.
~~~
