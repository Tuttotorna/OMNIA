# OMNIA — Master Position

## What OMNIA Is

OMNIA is not an AI model.

OMNIA is not a reasoning engine.

OMNIA is not a semantic evaluator.

OMNIA is not a truth oracle.

OMNIA is a post-hoc structural measurement framework.

Its purpose is to measure how much structure survives under:

- perturbation
- transformation
- representation change
- observer variation
- constraint pressure
- trajectory divergence

Core principle:

```text
structural truth = invariance under transformation

OMNIA measures structural behavior.

It does not decide meaning.

It does not decide truth.

It does not replace judgment.
